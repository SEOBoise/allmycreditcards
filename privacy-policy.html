Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1/12/2025

Welcome to allmycreditcards.com, your trusted source for fuel and gas credit card information. Protecting your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: When you contact us, subscribe to our newsletter, or submit inquiries, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Usage Data: We gather non-personal data, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system, to understand user behavior and improve our website.
  • Cookies: Our site uses cookies to enhance user experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our website
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To send newsletters or promotional materials (with your consent)
  • To analyze website usage and improve our services
  • To prevent fraud and ensure website security

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share data with trusted third-party service providers to help us operate the website, conduct business, or provide services, provided they agree to keep your information confidential.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before submitting personal data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional communications
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you

To exercise your rights, please contact us at:
Email: contact@allmycreditcards.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
